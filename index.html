Who was known as "The Greatest" in boxing history?

Muhammad Ali was known as "The Greatest" because of his exceptional skills, charisma, and impact both inside and outside the ring.

Which boxer retired with an undefeated record and was famous for his defensive style?

Floyd Mayweather Jr. retired undefeated with a 50–0 record and was renowned for his brilliant defense and counterpunching.

Who was the youngest heavyweight champion in boxing history?

Mike Tyson became the youngest heavyweight champion at the age of 20.

Which boxer won world titles in eight different weight divisions?

Manny Pacquiao is the only boxer in history to win world titles in eight weight classes.

Who defeated Wladimir Klitschko in 2015 to become the heavyweight champion?

Tyson Fury defeated Wladimir Klitschko in 2015 and ended Klitschko’s long reign as champion.
